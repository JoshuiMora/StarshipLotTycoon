# Starship Lot Tycoon

Starship Lot Tycoon is a business management simulation game where players restore a struggling starship dealership into the premier ship retailer in the galaxy.

Unlike traditional tycoon games that immediately focus on automation and expansion, Starship Lot Tycoon emphasizes making meaningful business decisions. Every ship purchased is an investment, every repair is a choice, and every sale is an opportunity to grow your reputation.

## Vision

Create a relaxing, rewarding business simulator that captures the feeling of building something from nothing.

Players begin with an abandoned hangar, a tiny office, and enough money to purchase their first broken ship. Through smart purchasing, careful restoration, customer negotiations, and financial planning, they slowly grow into the owner of a thriving dealership.

The game aims to encourage entrepreneurial thinking by rewarding planning, calculated risk, and long-term decision making.

## Core Gameplay Loop

Browse Listings
→ Purchase Ship
→ Delivery
→ Inspect
→ Create Restoration Plan
→ Order Parts
→ Restore Ship
→ Prepare for Sale
→ Customer Negotiation
→ Profit
→ Expand Business

## Design Pillars

- Business ownership over automation
- Meaningful decisions over busywork
- Cozy pacing over constant pressure
- Player agency over random outcomes
- Long-term progression that feels earned
